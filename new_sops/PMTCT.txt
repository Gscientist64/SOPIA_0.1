The four pillars of PMTCT:
Pillar 1 (Primary Prevention):
Low knowledge and risk perception
Late ANC start
Interventions: community-level HIV education, provider-facilitated screening, peer counselling
Pillar 2 (Preventing Unintended Pregnancy):
High rates of unintended pregnancy and unsafe abortions
Poor contraceptive access
Interventions: integrate contraception and dual protection in HIV care, create adolescent-friendly services
Pillar 3 (Preventing HIV Transmission):
Delayed ANC and ART initiation
Interventions: early case finding, testing, and ART initiation, youth-friendly service models
Pillar 4 (Treatment, Care, and Support):
Issues with adherence and child spacing
Interventions: integrate ART, sexual and reproductive health, and family planning; promote male and family involvement
